About Jack
I am a PhD candidate in philosophy at the University of Florida. 

I work primarily in applied ethics and also have significant interests in epistemology and political philosophy.

Before coming to UF, I completed an MA in philosophy at KU Leuven and a BA at DePaul University. 		jackmadock@gmail.com
