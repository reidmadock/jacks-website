I am a PhD candidate in philosophy at the University of Florida. 

I work primarily in applied ethics, especially ethics of technology. I also have significant interests in epistemology and political philosophy.

Before coming to UF, I completed an MA at KU Leuven and a BA at DePaul University. Under the research tab you will find links to my pubilshed paper. Under the teaching tab you will find courses I have taught and sample syllabi. 		jackmadock@gmail.com
